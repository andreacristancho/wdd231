Overview

Purpose

To provide online information related to the stock market, also known as the securities market, about buying and selling stocks, bonds and other financial instruments.

Audience

Individuals or companies that have a particular interest in how stocks are valued in order to be informed or because they have investments in them.

Scenarios

- How costly and time-consuming can it be for a company to regularly share information with its stakeholders?
